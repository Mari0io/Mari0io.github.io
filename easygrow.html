Some of the Easiest Flowers to Grow

Daffodils

W7-9 W10-12 W13-14
Growth Length 1-3 inches per week 3-6 inches per week full height (usually 6-24 inches, depending on the variety).

Why are they Easy to Grow?

Low-maintenance, and adaptable. thrive in various soil types, require minimal watering, and naturally return each year without replanting. making them ideal for beginners.

Poppies

W3-6 W7-10 W11-12
Growth Length 1-3 inches per week 3-6 inches per week Poppies reach their full height (12-48 inches, depending on variety)

Why are they Easy to Grow?

They grow quickly from seed, are drought-tolerant, and resistant to pests. Once established, they return year after year with little maintenance.

Daisies

W3-6 W7-10 W11-14
Growth Length 1-2 inches per week 2-4 inches per week Daisies reach their full height (6-36 inches, depending on variety).

Why are they Easy to Grow?

Their ability to flourish in both full sun and partial shade makes them versatile and easy to maintain, making them a great choice for beginner gardeners.

Lavender

W5-12 W13-24 W25+
Growth Length 0.5-1 inch per week. 1-3 inches per week. Lavender reaches its full height (12-36 inches, depending on variety).

Why are they Easy to Grow?

It needs minimal watering, loves full sun, and requires little maintenance once established.
